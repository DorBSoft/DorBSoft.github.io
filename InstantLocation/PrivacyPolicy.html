Privacy Policy for "Instant Location: View & Share"

At DorBSoft, we respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting their personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use that information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from our users of "Instant Location: View & Share". We do not require our users to create an account or provide any personal information to use our app.

Data Storage

We do not store any personal information on our servers. We do not collect device locations on our servers.

"Instant Location: View & Share" accesses your location only while using the app. This data is cached on your device for a limited time after locations have been visited. The location data cache is used locally on your device for better app performance and user experience. This location data cache is encrypted, and it is not transmitted to our servers.

DorBSoft location link

Depending on app settings, "Instant Location: View & Share" may generate a DorBSoft link when you request to share your location. Clicking on this link temporarily sends your location to our server to allow your intended audience to view it. The server processes this information momentarily and does not retain the location data, nor is it associated with any user personally or anonymously.

Third-Party Services

Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, such as device information and interactions with our application. This helps us understand how users engage with our app and make necessary improvements. We do not use Firebase Analytics to collect or store device locations.

Crash Reporting

We collect information about app crashes, which helps us identify and fix issues. This information is collected anonymously and does not include any personal information.

Advertising

"Instant Location: View & Share" contains advertising, which may be personalized based on your interests and other information we collect, as described in our Privacy Choices section.

Privacy Choices

You may choose to opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings or through other mechanisms as may be available on your device. Please note that opting out of personalized advertising does not mean you will no longer see ads, but rather that the ads you see may be less relevant to your interests.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users should review this policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or about "Instant Location: View & Share", please contact us at info@dorbsoft.com.
